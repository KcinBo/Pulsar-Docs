# Glossary

## APY

Annual percentage yield. A projection that compounds a current periodic reward rate over one year. It is not a guaranteed return.

## Backing

Treasury value attributed to the token under a stated calculation. Backing is not a peg or redemption promise.

## Bonding

The historical Olympus term for exchanging assets with a protocol for vested native tokens. Pulsar uses the friendlier term **minting** while acknowledging the bond-like economics.

## Circulating supply

Tokens considered available in the market under the protocol's published methodology.

## Epoch

A recurring interval at which the staking system or policy performs a scheduled update, such as a rebase.

## Emissions

Newly issued tokens. Emissions can distribute ownership but are not external revenue.

## Mint

An offer to exchange an approved asset or liquidity position for a quoted PULSAR payout, potentially subject to vesting.

## Net asset value (NAV)

Counted asset value minus counted liabilities under a stated methodology.

## pDAI

A colloquial name used for a DAI representation on PulseChain. The exact accepted token and contract must always be verified because tickers and names are not unique.

## Protocol-controlled value (PCV)

Assets the protocol controls, subject to its contracts, governance, custody, and liabilities.

## Protocol-owned liquidity (POL)

Liquidity positions owned by the treasury.

## PULSAR

The liquid native token of Pulsar Protocol.

## QUASAR

The staked representation of PULSAR.

## Rebase

An accounting update that proportionally changes eligible staking balances or their conversion value.

## Realized earnings

Income or gains actually received or recognized, less applicable realized costs and losses.

## Reward yield

The estimated percentage increase allocated to eligible stakers during one epoch.

## Runway

A scenario estimate of how long a current policy might continue under specified assumptions.

## Treasury

The collection of assets, positions, and liabilities controlled by the protocol.

## Vesting

The schedule over which a mint payout becomes claimable.

## Wrapped QUASAR

A planned non-rebasing token that may represent indexed QUASAR. Its final name and implementation are pending.

## (3,3)

A cultural shorthand for aligned participation, updated by Pulsar to emphasize building and compounding productive protocol capital.
